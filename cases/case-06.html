Case Study

Awkward or Cute

Creator/Organization: ComicCola Year: Type: Social Comic Why selected: Showcasing crowd-sourced content in visual form, focusing on Vietnamese story submissions

Context & Objective

This creator takes submissions from the audience and illustrate them as comic pages. These stories are mainly about love, surrounding all kinds of topic about love, hence why the audience is very intrigued by this premise of honest, everyday love stories, illustrated in a beautiful format.

Images

The approach is very clever, taking stories from the audience, and they can choose to illustrate the most interesting ones. This helps to show how personal everyday stories can be engaging content, enabling others to also submit theirs to be featured as comic. The artist is also very experienced, as their style perfectly capture the subtlety of love stories. And the stories are all from Vietnamese audience, so it shows how both young and old Vietnamese express love or discussing love stories through their submissions.

Key Takeaways

  • Crowd-sourcing content and featuring is feasible.
  • Artistic interpretation of personal stories make it more engaging for the audience, encouraging more to submit their own stories.

Reflection

This is one possible method for Design brief 2, for when we collect stories and try to feature them in a public format.
