Case Study

The Forever Labyrinth

Creator/Organization: Google Arts & Culture Year: 2024 Type: Web-based Game Why selected: An exploration experience to learn more about world history and culture

Context & Objective

The Forever Labyrinth is an ever-changing maze of halls, rooms, clifftops and cellars, filled with the collected works of the entire of the late human race. As we try to escape the maze, we encounter characters, interact and learn more about the items in the maze. And the game invites replays to unlock all its secrets.

Images

Very interesting premise to use a maze exploration/escape type to encourage learning about history and culture. Also, the style is very fitting with the game, and the whole experience is beautifully done. The game is also a replayable activity, allowing for more discovery to be made.

Key Takeaways

  • History and culture can be gamified in fun and engaging way.
  • Encoding information and knowledge as secrets in the game sparks curiousity in players, motivating them to keep playing.

Reflection
