Case Study

Never Alone

Creator/Organization: Upper One Games Year: 2014 Type: Puzzle-platform adventure video game Why selected: Community-based Approach in Game Design

Context & Objective

Never Alone is an atmospheric puzzle platformer developed in collaboration with the IÃ±upiat, an Alaska Native people, drawn from a traditional story that has been shared across the generations. This work shows how game designers can co-create narratives with the community.

Images

The narrative and theme of the game is inspired the Indigenous Alaskan culture, and the game designers worked with over 40 community members when creating this game to ensure accurate depiction of their culture.

Key Takeaways

  • Complex game narratives about cultural heritage can and should be co-designed with the community of that culture.
  • Game offer a promising venue to visual depict stories and mythologies in an engaging way that can introduce the culture to a wider audience.

Reflection

I really like the approach of these game designers in co-creating with the community to ensure accurate and respectful depiction of their culture. And the game itself is beautiful and shows how mythologies can be integrated into games.
