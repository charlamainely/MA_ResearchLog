Case Study

Storyteller

Creator/Organization: Daniel Benmergui Year: 2023 Type: Interactive Storytelling, Narrative-based Mechanics Why selected: Fairytale-based content and storytelling mechanics

Context & Objective

Storyteller is a puzzle game in which players form story narratives by placing characters and scenes into picture book styledÂ panels. This game allows players to question existing fairytale structures and reinterpret them as their own through their choices in building the stories.

Images

This game makes us critically analyze the narrative structure of well-known fairytales, and allow players to reconstruct the stories in their own way, so long as it satisfy the plot. This way, players can exercise their creativity when building the narrative, but based on what they've already known.

Key Takeaways

  • Fairytales offer an interesting content to be gamified and reinterpreted.
  • Players enjoy the agency of telling the story on their own terms, but still has to satisfy narrative constraints and logic.

Reflection

The game offers just the perfect amount of freedom and constraint in the game design to make it exciting for players to engage and solve these narrative puzzles, but still has narrative rules in there to enforce narrative logic and structure.
