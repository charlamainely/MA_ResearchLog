Reflection

Though the formative feedback presentation as well as the critical discourse consultation weren't marked, I tried to come prepare with everything I had, which led to me not quite making it within the time limit. But the feedback really helped me realize which information was important for which presentation, and how I can trim down the information so that it's more concise and towards the point. And because I was too content-focused, I neglected the visual aspect of the presentation, which I received from feedback on. Hence why I would also invest more time in designing the slides to keep it both informative but also visually appealing. Dr. Aprille advice on how to approach the critical discourse using the handwriting technique as well as the funnel also really helped, and I tried using her approach and mapped out all the literature I've read using keywords to anchor me. Through that I was able to identify key areas to focus on and who were the most influential authors. It also helped me see the progression of the discourse and where the literature in different areas converges.
