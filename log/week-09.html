Reflection

The track workshop really gave me a new experience on how others view my research topic. I think this perception would be determined based on how well I explain it, and how clearly defined my topic is. But based on my current explanation, my classmate has been able to produce some interesting ideas that I would definitely consider for my research.

And the different situational prompts also allowed me to see my topic through different lens, and see how I would approach it if there weren't any practical constraints. As for the presentation, I took down notes so that I can expand on them in my essay. However, the feedback about grounding the research in Vietnamese context is very important, as so far, I haven't included many Vietnamese articles or scholarly sources. So I will look into having more of these for the essay.
