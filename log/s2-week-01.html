Reflection

After a long break period, I finally sat down this week to compile all my research findings, insights and test data into a presentation, which not only helped me clarify my new research focus but also creates the foundation for my Seminar presentation in Week 3. Though I feel a bit nervous about such a big shift in direction, it's not exactly a change in topic, but more so a deeper inquiry into my previous topic of 'cultural preservation via storytelling'. My previous framing took for granted the required conditions for success, and through testing my prototype, I identify a much more dire and urgent ongoing issue that requires intervention, which is the generational difference existing in society today that prevents intergenerational dialogue, and hence, prevents the cultural exchange that could lead to cultural preservation. Hence, my project now hopes to create the condition for change, and can hopefully enable more conversations and empathy between generations.
