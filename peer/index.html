Clarifying branching prompts for folktale choices

2025-09-08 People: Ava · Ken Tags: prototype folktales branching

Context

Peer review of the prototype’s choice points. The current labels (“A path opens”) were read as too abstract.

Discussion & Insights

  • Use verb-led prompts: “Ask elder”, “Follow footprints”, “Return to village”.
  • Surface variant paths as “other tellings” to respect ICH diversity without overwhelming.
  • Audio narration helps non-readers; captions help noisy environments.

Reflection

I’ll keep branching but tighten language and add an audio toggle. For accessibility, I’ll test a minimum 16px body size and contrast against dark image backgrounds.

Next Steps

  1. Rewrite 6 prompts with verbs + nouns, test comprehension with 3–5 users.
  2. Prototype “other tellings” drawer for variants.
  3. Add audio on/off and measure load time on mobile.
